## 🚀 Key Business Insights
* The Growth Engine (+54.3% YoY): Total sales achieved an impressive $19.79M, heavily sustained by optimized individual sales performances and strong regional traction—proving that micro-level efficiency is driving macro success.
* Geographic & Product Dominance: Granular decomposition traces revenue directly to its roots, pinpointing Australia as the powerhouse in sales volume, with *Mallorie Waber* emerging as the top-performing sales representative.
* Product Portfolio Optimization: Premium dark chocolate variants dominate the top slots, led by market favorites like *Smooth Silky Salty* and *50% Dark Bites*, giving clear indicators of market preferences.
* Operational Health: Maintained a high-value average of $6.03K per sales transaction, pointing towards highly consolidated bulk orders and wholesale distribution efficiency rather than fragmented retail sales.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Data Engineering
* Data Sourcing: Sourced a global retail transaction dataset from Kaggle to deep dive into realistic multi-year metrics.
* Data Modeling: Structured a clean dimensional schema to seamlessly handle thousands of transactional rows and multiple dimensions.
* Analytics & DAX: Formulated advanced Time Intelligence measures to track YoY growth trajectories and smooth out seasonal trend noise.
* UI/UX Design: Executed a cohesive, presentation-ready layout with custom formatting optimized for corporate readability.
