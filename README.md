# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Performance Dashboard (2022–2025)

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to transform global chocolate sales data into actionable business insights. This project focuses on sales performance analysis, product trends, geographic distribution, and sales team effectiveness through a clean and executive-friendly reporting experience.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of sales performance from 2022 to 2025, enabling stakeholders to monitor key metrics and identify performance trends quickly.

<img src="Chocolate Sales Performance.jpg" width="100%" alt="Dashboard Preview">

---

## 🚀 Key Insights

### 💰 Sales Performance
* Total sales reached **$19.79M** across the analyzed period.
* Sales trends were visualized to highlight performance changes over time and support data-driven decision-making.

### 🌍 Geographic Analysis
* Sales performance is mapped by country, allowing users to identify regional strengths and opportunities for growth.
* Interactive visuals provide a clear view of global sales distribution.

### 🍫 Product Performance
* Top-selling products were identified through comparative analysis.
* Product rankings help uncover customer preferences and support inventory and marketing decisions.

### 👥 Sales Team Performance
* Sales representative performance metrics allow managers to evaluate productivity and monitor contribution levels across the team.
